Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

PropChat ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our WhatsApp CRM platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, phone number, and business details when you register.
  • Contact Data: Contact lists you upload or import into the platform for broadcasting and CRM purposes. You are the Data Controller of this data.
  • Usage Data: Broadcast analytics, message delivery reports, login activity, and feature usage.
  • Payment Information: Billing details processed securely through our third-party payment provider. We do not store your credit card information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the PropChat platform and services.
  • To process message credits and manage your account.
  • To generate analytics and performance reports for your dashboard.
  • To send service-related notifications (billing alerts, limit warnings, feature updates).
  • To comply with legal obligations under Malaysian law, including the Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA).

3. Data Processing Role

PropChat acts as a Data Processor. You (the user) are the Data Controller of any contact data you upload or manage through our platform. You are responsible for ensuring that you have obtained proper consent from your contacts before sending them messages, in compliance with the PDPA and WhatsApp's policies.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Your data is stored on secure cloud servers with encryption at rest and in transit.
  • We implement industry-standard security measures including access controls, audit logs, and regular security reviews.
  • Data is retained for the duration of your active subscription. Upon account deletion, your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

5. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party services to operate our platform:

  • Meta (WhatsApp Business API): For message delivery. Subject to WhatsApp's Privacy Policy.
  • Payment Processors: For secure payment handling.
  • Cloud Infrastructure: For hosting and data storage.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Your Rights (PDPA Malaysia)

Under the Personal Data Protection Act 2010, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data held by us.
  • Correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and preferences. We do not use third-party tracking cookies for advertising.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or through the platform. Continued use of PropChat after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
